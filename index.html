Est. in the Heart of London

The House of
Paddington

◆

Nestled in the storied streets of Paddington, London, The House of Paddington is far more than a souvenir shop — it is a lovingly curated sanctuary of British heritage, timeless charm, and authentic artistry. Founded with a deep reverence for the stories that have shaped this great city, we bring together an exquisite collection of keepsakes, gifts, and treasures that honour the spirit of London in every detail.

From hand-selected Paddington Bear mementos — each bearing the warmth and whimsy that have made the beloved bear an icon of Britishness — to finely crafted memorabilia celebrating London's royal history, iconic landmarks, and enduring culture, every piece in our collection has been chosen with discernment and care.

We believe that a truly special souvenir is not merely an object, but a vessel of memory — a piece of London carried home in the hands and held in the heart. Step inside, and allow us to help you find yours.

— The House of Paddington
